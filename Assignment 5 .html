Top 4 Beaches in Malvan 🌴

Tarkarli Beach 🐠

Tarkarli is famed for its crystal-clear waters, scuba diving, and snorkeling. It’s a hotspot for thrill-seekers and nature lovers, with colorful coral reefs and dolphin-spotting boat rides. 📸
📍 Location: 6 km south of Malvan town, Maharashtra

Dandi Beach 🌊

Perfect for peaceful strolls and golden hour views, Dandi Beach offers clean shores and an untouched ambiance. Enjoy a scenic view of Sindhudurg Fort from here. 🌅
📍 Location: 3 km from Malvan city center

Devbagh Beach 🐚

This tranquil beach lies at the river-sea confluence, perfect for boating and birdwatching. A favorite among solitude seekers and nature photographers. 🦜
📍 Location: Near Karli River estuary, 12 km from Malvan

Chivla Beach 🌅

Chivla is a quiet, less commercial beach popular with locals. It’s perfect for family outings, swimming, and enjoying delicious seafood from nearby shacks. 🐟
📍 Location: Just west of Malvan city, walkable distance
